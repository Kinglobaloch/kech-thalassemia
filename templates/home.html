Frequently Asked Questions

KTCC stands for Kech Thalassemia Care Center. We are a first free healthcare provider for thallassemia disease in the region. Being operated through donations and charity.

Thalassemia is a blood disorder passed down through families (inherited) in which the body makes an abnormal form or inadequate amount of hemoglobin.

You can donate blood by visiting our center or during our blood donation camps. Please contact us for more details.
